About Refodile Clinic

Refodile Clinic is a proud family-owned medical clinic, passionately led by a registered nurse specializing in the delicate field of neonatology. We are committed to providing exceptional care for our community.

We offer a comprehensive range of services and accept both medical aid schemes and out-of-pocket payments to ensure healthcare is accessible to everyone.
